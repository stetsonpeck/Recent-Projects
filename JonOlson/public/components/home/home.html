My name is Jonathan Olson and I am a local artist and designer from Salt Lake City, Utah. I made this blog to showcase my art and have a place for people to hopefully get ideas or inspiration from the things I create. I am constantly trying to learn from others and pull inspiration from anything I can. I have a beautiful wife and son that are my whole world. Everything I do is for them.
